FINAL FANTASY RECORD KEEPER



WHAT IS FFRK

Final Fantasy Record Keeper, also known as FFRK, is a mobile game developed by DENA and published by Square-Enix. It is a 2D 8 bit style RPG allowing players to relive stories of past Final Fantasy games in 8 bit style.

FFRK is also what's known as a Gacha game in Japan. Meaning players collect a currency through doing stuff and spend them on weekly events to get new stuff. So players can collect the currency known as Mythril by doing battles in the game and spend them on powerful equipment for certain characters.

Players can collect Characters freely, fight 2D 8 bit battles, create special attacks and collect equipment. There's a ton of content available to keep users busy for new and veteran players alike FFRK is free to download on Android and IOS and also free to play but be warned there are micro transactions on offer as well.
