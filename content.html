WHAT IS FINAL FANTASY


Final Fantasy is a Japanese Role Playing, also known as a JRPG, game franchise created by Square Enix. The First game released in Japan in 1987 and was created by Hironboi Sagaguchi.

It was never intended to be a franchise but the first game did so well that the company decided to make another. The next game was not a direct sequal to the first but introduced a whole new story and content but was still a JRPG style game and retained the title Final Fantasy .

After that a new game was released almost every year with a new number after it and Final Fantasy has become one of the biggest JRPG game franchise in the world.
